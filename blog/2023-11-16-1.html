StaTech Industry Part 4

Basebuilding

Time to build the actual base. This is going to be an underground base, centered around a "main room", containing a water elevator for surface access and the various manual crafting machines. Tunnelling outwards in the cardinal directions are four wings - industry, magic, food, and miscellaneous.

First up was digging the water elevator down to y = -50, and constructing the hut at the top.

Next was filling the elevator, after a brief diversion to a local meteor site for some soul sand and magma. This was a lengthy process that I'm glad I only have to do once.

Next was digging out the planned rooms in the industrial wing - a double-wide room for the power plant, a double wide storage room, four single-wide rooms for basic refining, advanced refining, basic manufacturing, and assembling, another single-wide room for quarries, a quadruple-wide room for multiblock processing machines, a double wide room for oil processing, and another double wide room for the two large multiblocks.

And finally, I moved everything down into the permanent base.
