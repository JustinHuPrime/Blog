StaTech Industry Part 11

We're In The Endgame Now

We're in the endgame now. Today, we make some singularities, and start replication.

First up was building the quasi-quantum singularity forge.

Next was getting three singularities - two for the quantum upgrades for the replicator, and one for UU matter production. The first one I made was for UU matter production.

And the next two were for quantum upgrades, which were immediately crafted into a replicator.

Then, to upgrade my UU-matter production, I made a quantum upgrade, using my replicator. My factory was now 99% redundant - I needed power production, but other than that, I could replicate anything I needed. I needed just the creative tank to get infinite UU matter, which was just over an hour worth of production from a single electrolyzer. The replicator blacklist, alas, appears to have been sufficiently well curated that I couldn't get around this, so I applied some time in a bottle.

I made a slight mistake while crafting the creative tank - I should have hooked up my assembler to the fusion reactor instead of my workshop's power grid. Oh well - something to do for the other creative components.

Next was a creative storage unit, a copy of which I hooked up to the superconductor power grid for the assembler.

I then made the other creative components - the barrel and the motor. The infinity sword and suit took a bit more microcrafting. One last replication, and I got the creative worldshaper.

And with that, I've finished StaTech Industry.
