StaTech Industry Part 10

Going nuclear

Today, we go nuclear, both nuclear fission and nuclear fusion.

I set up the reactor - it's fuelled by natural uranium, and the inner area is low-pressure light water cooled. There's an outer jacket of low-pressure heavy water for tritium production. I decided to place it in the advanced processing room since its primary purpose wasn't really energy generation - it was plutonium production.

Next, I built out the superconductor cable production chain, then the space probe production chain - the desh drill part would be useful for the core mining drill, and the ostrum would be useful for blastproof plates and nuclear alloy plates.

I next set up the quantum circuit production chain and the highly advanced upgrade production chain, and then wandered off into the twilight forest as the factory processed things. There, I fought the Minoshroom.

I kept wandering around - I was going to go fight the hydra next. Hopefully my fire resistance would help here. It did - I was completely immune to the ranged attacks of the hydra.

Next up, the Alpha Yeti. Fall damage immunity really helped here.

Deeper in I went, petting the penguins along the way - I was headed to the snow queen's palace.

Next, I headed for the knight phantoms and the carminite tower.

The tower was pretty tall and intimidating, but I had a gravichestplate.

The factory had fininshed producing resources at this point, so I went around and upgraded everything to EV hulls. While I was waiting for the fusion reactor components to process, I went into the twilight forest again, and visited the questing ram.

I then hunted some trolls in the highlands.

Afterwards, I planted the magic beans, and headed up the beanstalk to the giants' home.

Having gotten the giant pickaxe, I went down and broke into the trolls' obsidian vault, and took the lamp of cinders and started burning my way to the final castle.

Alas, there did my journey end, for the castle has no boss to fight. There was no more progression past here.

Although, just what was in the basement of the castle? A whole bunch of cells, apparently.

Next, I went back and set up the core mining drill, along with a fusion reactor.

The fusion reactor is primarily intended for neutronium production - I'll be adding a second ring on top later to make the neutronium. I next set up the core mining drill.

Most of it was buried underground. With access to core fragments, I now put together the processing chain for ultradense metal balls. Next, I built the neutronium fusion reactor.

Next time, the singularity forge and endgame.
